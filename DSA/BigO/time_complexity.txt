Big O notation:
what is O(n^3) 
this is the max realtionship between the time and number of inputs. It could have taken O(n^2) or O(n) but the O(n^3) is the upper bound. 


Time complexity is the realtionship about how the time grows as the input grows : https://youtu.be/mV3wrLBbuuE?si=8730I8iNpC2Sxc4m&t=722

what to consider when thinking about complexity:
1. Worst case
2. Look for large data (as the small data does not give the trend of the algorithm we need large data to consider the trend)
3. Ignore the constants (constants only give the amplitude but not the trend. Always think in graph terms)
4. Ignore the less the dominant terms
